Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our vehicle towing and junk car services

Do you use a tow truck or something else?

+

We use a 16-foot flatbed trailer with a winch, NOT a traditional tow truck. This is safer for your vehicle and allows us to securely transport cars, trucks, and other vehicles without additional damage. Our flatbed setup is perfect for non-running vehicles and ensures safe transport.

What types of vehicles do you buy for cash?

+

We buy ALL types of vehicles: cars, trucks, vans, and SUVs. Running or not running, wrecked, flooded, or in perfect condition - we buy them all! We even purchase vehicles with missing parts, expired registration, or no title (in some cases).

How quickly can you pick up my vehicle?

+

We offer same-day pickup in most cases! For junk car purchases, we can typically arrive within 2-4 hours. For emergency towing situations, we prioritize urgent calls and can often be there even faster. We work around YOUR schedule.

Do I need a title to sell my junk car?

+

While having a title makes the process easier, we can still buy your vehicle in many cases without one. We'll work with you to handle the paperwork properly. Requirements vary by vehicle age and condition, so call us to discuss your specific situation.

Is the towing really free when you buy my car?

+

YES! Towing is 100% FREE when we purchase your vehicle. There are no hidden fees, towing charges, or surprise deductions. The cash amount we quote you is exactly what you'll receive. We handle all towing costs ourselves.

How do you determine how much to pay for my junk car?

+

We consider several factors: vehicle make, model, year, condition, weight, current scrap metal prices, and location. We pay competitive rates based on current market values. Plus, leave us a Google review and get an extra 10% bonus on your payout!

Can you tow my car if it's not running or has flat tires?

+

Absolutely! Our 16-foot flatbed trailer with winch is specifically designed for non-running vehicles. Flat tires, no brakes, seized engine, or any other mechanical issues - we can safely load and transport your vehicle. The winch pulls it right onto the flatbed.

What are the maximum specifications for vehicles you can tow?

+

Our 16-foot flatbed trailer with winch can handle most standard vehicles. We can tow cars, trucks, vans, and SUVs within reasonable size and weight limits. Our equipment is designed for vehicles up to approximately 10,000 lbs and standard passenger vehicle dimensions. For oversized vehicles, commercial trucks, or extremely heavy equipment, please call us to discuss your specific needs - we may be able to arrange special equipment or refer you to appropriate services.

What areas do you serve for pickup and towing?

+

We serve Fernandina Beach, Yulee, Jacksonville, St. Marys, Kingsland, Woodbine, and surrounding areas in Nassau County (FL), Duval County (FL), and Camden County (GA). Call us to confirm service to your specific location.
